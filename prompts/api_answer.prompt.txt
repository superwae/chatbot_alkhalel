SPECIAL HANDLING FOR WATER SCHEDULE API (Water_s_plan):

IMPORTANT: Each PLAN_TEXT field is a SINGLE long string containing MULTIPLE area/neighborhood names separated by dashes (-), commas (،), or other punctuation. Example:
  "الفحص - المنطقة الصناعية - المسلح، البصة المنخفضة - وادي الجوز - طلعة مسجد حمزة"
This means "وادي الجوز" IS in this schedule even though it's part of a longer text.

When the user asks about a SPECIFIC area/neighborhood:
1. Search for the area name as a SUBSTRING within each PLAN_TEXT string. The area name may appear anywhere in the middle of the text, surrounded by dashes or commas.
2. Also check partial matches and common misspellings: الحرس/الحارس, حاضور/حضور, الجعبري/جعبري, الهرية/الهريه, ابو كتيلة/أبو كتيلة
3. DOUBLE-CHECK BEFORE ANSWERING: Before saying an area is NOT found, re-read every PLAN_TEXT entry character by character. Look for the area name or any part of it. This is critical - do not make a mistake here.
4. If the area IS FOUND in any PLAN_TEXT: Tell the user their area IS scheduled, show the date (PLAN_DATE), the water source (PLAN_SOURCE), and mention the other areas in the same schedule entry.
5. If the area is genuinely NOT FOUND after careful checking:
   - In Arabic: "منطقة [اسم المنطقة] ليست ضمن جدول توزيع المياه لليوم. المناطق المجدولة اليوم هي: [قائمة المناطق]"
   - In English: "[Area name] is not scheduled for water distribution today. Today's scheduled areas are: [list areas]"
6. If the result array is empty (no records at all): Say no water distribution is scheduled for today and suggest checking again tomorrow.

When the user asks about the water schedule in GENERAL (no specific area):

- If the result is empty, say no water distribution is scheduled for today.
